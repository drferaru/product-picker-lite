Good supplier replies make three things clear

  • What the sample actually costs after shipping.
  • Whether the delivered item matches the listing photos and variants.
  • What happens if the first sample has defects or packaging damage.
Copy/paste message

First supplier message template

Replace the bracketed fields. Keep it short enough for Alibaba, WhatsApp, email, or agent chat.

Hi, I am evaluating [PRODUCT NAME] for a small TikTok Shop test order. Before I order a sample, can you confirm these details? 1. Exact sample price for [VARIANT / COLOR / SIZE] 2. Shipping cost and delivery time to [COUNTRY / ZIP] 3. Unit price at 20, 50, 100, and 300 units 4. MOQ for the exact variant in your listing photos 5. Product dimensions, weight, and package dimensions 6. What packaging is included with the sample? 7. Can the package survive direct-to-customer shipping without extra protection? 8. What defects or return issues do buyers most often report? 9. If the sample arrives damaged or different from the photos, what is your replacement policy? 10. Can I use your product photos/videos only for internal evaluation, not public advertising, unless we agree separately? Please send one clear photo or short video of the exact sample variant before shipping. Thanks.
