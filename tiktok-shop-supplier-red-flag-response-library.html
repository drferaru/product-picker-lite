Red flags to answer once, not negotiate forever

  • "MOQ 500 only" before sample proof.
  • Shipping cost hidden until after payment.
  • No packaging or defect replacement policy.
  • Variant photos do not match the listing.
  • Supplier avoids one simple scale or use-case photo.

Copyable message

Use this as the second message

Thanks. Before I pay for a sample, please confirm these five things:
1. Total landed sample cost to my address.
2. Exact sample quantity and variant/SKU.
3. Packaging photo and one scale/use-case photo.
4. What happens if the sample arrives damaged or missing parts.
5. Whether the sample photos and video can be used for internal product testing.

If all five are clear, I can decide on the sample. If not, I will pause this product for now.

Use the reply as evidence. A supplier that answers cleanly can move into sample QC. A supplier that keeps dodging basics should not get a bulk order or creator spend.
