Reject fast

If you cannot storyboard three angles, do not rush the sample order.

Simple rule: a first product should have at least three visual tests before inventory. If the product only works with a long explanation, it probably belongs back in the scorecard.

  • Reject products where the benefit is invisible on camera.
  • Reject products where the only hook depends on exaggerated results.
  • Reject products where the demo creates obvious return or expectation risk.
  • Keep products where the proof is physical, simple, and repeatable.
