The pre-sample compliance and risk checklist

CheckPass signalReject signal
CategoryEveryday consumer product with clear allowed use.Restricted, regulated, or unclear category.
ClaimsDemo can show physical benefit without promises.Needs health, income, safety, weight-loss, or cure wording.
Supplier proofSupplier can send specs, packaging photos, variant list, and sample terms.Only sends hype, screenshots, or pressure to order bulk.
PackagingShips safely, looks presentable, and survives normal handling.Fragile, leaky, unlabeled, or expensive to protect.
ReturnsDefects are easy to identify and replacement terms are clear.High defect ambiguity or customer-use damage disputes.
Content proofOne honest 3-second before/after or use-case shot sells the idea.Needs fake UGC, fake results, or long explanation.
Margin after riskMargin still survives sample, shipping, platform fees, returns, and creators.Only profitable in the supplier quote screenshot.

Fast rule: if the product needs a risky claim to sell, reject it for a first TikTok Shop test. A clean demo with plain benefits is easier to source, film, and support.

Copyable supplier risk question

Send this before paying for the sample

“Before I order a sample, please confirm the exact product material, dimensions, packaging, variant options, battery or liquid status if applicable, defect/replacement policy, sample price, shipping cost to the US, and whether your product photos/videos can be used for a small TikTok Shop test. Please also send one packaging photo and one close-up product photo.”

If the supplier cannot answer this cleanly, score the product as reject or watch, not sample-ready.
