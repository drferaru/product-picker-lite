  • Wrong variant, color, bundle, plug, size, or packaging.

Copy-paste message

Supplier replacement request

Hi [Supplier Name], The sample arrived, but I cannot approve a reorder yet because of these issues: 1. Defect or mismatch: [describe the exact defect] 2. Photo/video proof: [attach filename or list attached photos] 3. Listing/spec mismatch: [variant, size, material, packaging, color, bundle, or function] 4. Business impact: this creates buyer refund/return risk and content demo risk. Please confirm these four points before I make a reorder decision: A. Will you send a replacement sample or refund this sample cost? B. What caused the defect or mismatch? C. What exact QC or packaging step will prevent this in the next unit? D. Can you send a fresh photo/video of the corrected unit, packaging, and variant label before shipment? If you can solve this clearly, I can continue evaluating the product. If not, I will reject this supplier/product before inventory spend. Thanks, [Your Name]
Good reply
Specific cause, replacement/refund option, corrected packaging proof, clear variant confirmation.
Watch reply
Supplier offers discount but does not explain the defect or show the corrected next unit.
Reject reply
“This is normal,” “buy more first,” “do not worry,” or any answer that skips replacement terms.
Decision rule

Do not let one bad sample turn into bad inventory.

If the sample fails but the supplier gives a precise, accountable fix, keep evaluating. If the reply stays vague, reject before reorder. Product Picker Lite gives you the larger go/no-go filter around margin, content proof, supplier clarity, return risk, and first order size.
