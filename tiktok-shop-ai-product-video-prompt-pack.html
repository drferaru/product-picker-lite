Prompt 2, UGC-style demo: Create a vertical demo-style clip from a neutral product-overview perspective, not a fake customer review. Show [product] being used to solve [problem]. First frame: [before state]. Middle: [hands/use/setup shot]. End frame: [clear result and CTA]. Keep it truthful and product-focused. Prompt 3, objection proof: Create a vertical clip that answers the main buyer objection: [size/fit/setup/durability/cleaning/compatibility]. Start with the objection visually, then show [proof shot]. Desk cable organizer prompt map

ConceptFirst framePrompt directionPass signal
Spec ad A messy desk with tangled cords crossing a keyboard. Show one hand clipping cables into a clean row, then a wide desk reset shot. The benefit is obvious with sound off in under 3 seconds.
UGC-style demo Closeup of cords falling behind a desk. Neutral product-overview clip showing setup, cable thickness, and final desk look. Viewer understands fit and setup without a fake testimonial.
Objection proof Organizer next to thick, thin, and braided cables. Show what fits, what does not, and how it attaches to the surface. Fewer questions about size, compatibility, or adhesive risk.
