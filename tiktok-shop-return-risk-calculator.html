Five return triggers to score before sourcing

1. Size or fit ambiguity Anything worn, mounted, or matched to body/home dimensions needs clearer buyer guidance than a quick viral clip usually gives.
2. Fragile or packaging-sensitive If the product needs perfect packaging to survive shipping, ask for real sample packaging proof before inventory.
3. Demo expectation gap If the video makes the result look instant, effortless, or cleaner than normal use, returns and angry comments can follow.
4. Material or variant confusion Same listing, different factory version, different texture, or unclear bundle contents can create refund pressure.
5. Claim or category friction Avoid first tests that depend on health, safety, beauty, battery, child, pet, or regulated performance claims.
6. Support burden If buyers will need setup help, troubleshooting, measurements, or replacements, margin has to survive that support time.
