Supplier message

Copyable defect follow-up

Hello, the sample arrived with the defect shown in the attached photos/video.

Before I consider another sample or small order, please confirm:
1. What caused this defect?
2. What packaging or QC step will prevent it on the next unit?
3. Will you replace this sample or credit the next sample?
4. Can you send a photo/video of the corrected packaging and one inspected unit before shipment?
5. If this happens in a small batch, what replacement/refund policy applies?

I am not ready for bulk MOQ until the sample issue is fixed and documented.

Rule: if the reply is vague, run it through the supplier reply scorecard before sending more money.
