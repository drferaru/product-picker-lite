Worked example

Desk cable organizer listing photos

Pass: main image shows the messy desk before/after, scale shot shows fit under a normal desk, detail shot shows adhesive or screw mount, and bundle shot shows the exact number of clips.

Reshoot before launch: supplier photos only show a perfect render on a white background. The buyer cannot see scale, adhesive strength, cable capacity, or what arrives in the box.
