Worked example

Desk cable organizer hook map

AngleOpening frameTest scriptPass signal
Problem Tangled charger cables sliding behind a desk. "If your charger disappears every time you unplug it, this tiny clip fixes the annoying part." Comments mention the same problem without being prompted.
Proof One hand snaps three cables into fixed clips. "Three cables, one strip, ten seconds. This is the whole demo." Viewers understand the benefit with sound off.
Comparison Split screen: messy cable pile versus clipped desk edge. "Old desk: cable pile. New desk: every cable stays where your hand reaches." Saves/shares indicate the visual setup is worth copying.
